Master Layout Plan

Education section aims to fulfill basic requirement behind creation of Zoo. It is to provide awareness and information regarding wildlife and different related issues. So essentially it has to do the following activities:

  1. Took into consideration all the rules of the Central Zoo Authority under the wildlife protection Act 1972.
  2. Worked on the flaws observed from the previous zoos built in India.
  3. A major plus point of our zoo’s layout is that, instead of a large number of loops, it only has one big ring road which comprises of enclosures on both sides of the path.
  4. On entering the zoo the visitor will visit the type of animals they see will be in order : Amphibians , carnivores, herbivores, reptiles and birds.
  5. An inbuilt burial ground as well as a veterinary clinic included within the premises of the zoo is beneficial to the sick animals and their immediate care.
  6. A rescue center for pets makes the zoo more inclusive of the type of animals coming in as well as fosters a larger variety of helpless animals.
  7. Staff headquarters is also considered for staff convenience.
  8. Entertainment with the stadium will include bird shows which will boost the city’s tourist and overall economic condition.
  9. With these minimal and basic requirements in addition with adhering to all zoo rules our zoo provides a fair depiction of what an ideal zoo would look like.

One of the objectives of Our Zoological Park is to provide unforgettable visitor experience. Inspire them to support and contribute to the cause of conservation of wildlife, habitat and water. Provide opportunities for passive recreation.
